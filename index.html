About Me

In 2007 I graduated with a BA and MEng in Chemical Engineering from Robinson College, University of Cambridge and joined the CoMo Group.

I am interested in modelling particle formation and transport in reactive flows by the coupling and development of algorithms to both commercial (STAR-CD) and open source (OpenFOAM) computational fluid dynamics (CFD) codes that are capable of handling complex geometries.
